point_of_sale Billing View - User Guide

Overview

The Billing View allows you to easily create product invoices, manage customer details, apply discounts, and process payments. It is designed to be fast and intuitive, ensuring a smooth checkout experience for your customers.

Key Sections

1. Customer Details

Capture and manage customer information for the invoice:

  • Phone Number: Enter the 10-digit phone number. The system will automatically look up existing customers and autofill their details.
  • Customer Name: Enter the full name of the customer.
  • Email & Address: Optional fields to capture the customer's contact information.
  • State: Select the customer's state for accurate GST calculation (e.g., CGST/SGST vs IGST).
  • GSTIN: Enter the customer's GST number if applicable.
  • Consignee Details: If the shipping address is different from the billing address, check the "Different Consignee" option to enter the shipping details.
  • SEZ Zone: Check this box if the customer is in a Special Economic Zone (SEZ), which will apply zero GST.
  • Save Customer: Check this box to save the new customer's details for future transactions.

2. Add Products

Add items to the customer's bill:

  • Add Product Button: Opens a list to select products. You can also use a barcode scanner if supported.
  • Quantity Adjustment: Use the + and - buttons to adjust the quantity. The system will warn you if you exceed the available stock.
  • Price Editing: Tap on the unit price to edit it. The system enforces a minimum price to ensure you do not sell below cost.
  • Tax Calculation: Taxes are automatically calculated based on the product's tax rate, customer's state, and SEZ status.

3. Discounts

  • Auto-Discounts: Any category or product-level discounts are automatically applied to the respective items.
  • Global Discount: You can apply a flat discount or percentage discount to the entire subtotal using the discount button.

4. Hold Bill (Drafts)

  • Pause Icon: If you need to temporarily stop billing (e.g., the customer forgot their wallet), tap the pause icon at the top right to hold the bill.
  • Retrieve Drafts: Held bills will appear in a yellow banner at the top of the screen. You can view, restore, or delete them at any time.

5. Payments

  • Proceed to Payment: Once all products are added, click this button to finalize the bill.
  • Payment Methods: You can select from various payment methods, including Cash, Card, Online Payment (Razorpay, Pinelabs, Paytm), or mark it as partially paid.

Help & Tips

  • Use the Help Icon (?) at the top right for quick reminders on how to use the screen.
  • Ensure your product inventory is updated to avoid "Exceeds Available Stock" warnings.
