pending_actions 15. Pending Payments Guide

The Pending Payments screen helps you keep track of outstanding dues from your customers. It acts as an accounts receivable ledger, listing all invoices that haven't been fully paid yet.

Key Features

  • Pending & Partial Invoices: Lists all invoices with a status of either PENDING (no payment recorded) or PARTIAL (some amount has been paid, but a balance remains).
  • Search Functionality: Quickly find specific invoices by searching for the Customer Name or Invoice Number.
  • Outstanding Balance View: Each card displays the total invoice amount alongside the remaining balance due.
  • Automated Bookkeeping: Payments made via Card or UPI are automatically logged to your Business Accounts Tracker.

Recording a Payment

When a customer pays their outstanding balance, you need to record it in the system:

  1. Locate the invoice on the Pending Payments screen.
  2. Click the Record Payment button on the invoice card.
  3. In the popup dialog, the "Amount to Pay" field will auto-fill with the total remaining balance.
    • For Full Payments: Leave the amount as is.
    • For Partial Payments: Modify the amount to reflect what the customer actually paid.
  4. Select the Payment Method (Cash, Card, or UPI) from the dropdown menu.
  5. Click Save Payment.

What happens next?

  • If the payment covers the entire remaining balance, the invoice status changes to Paid and it is automatically removed from this list.
  • If it was a partial payment, the invoice status changes to PARTIAL and the remaining "Balance Due" is updated.
  • Non-cash payments are securely recorded in your Business Tracker.
