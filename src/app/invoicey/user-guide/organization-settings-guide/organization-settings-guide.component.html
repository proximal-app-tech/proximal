settings 2. Organization Settings Guide

The Organization Settings section is the central hub for managing your business profile, subscription plans, and integrations within Invoicey. Keeping this information accurate ensures that your invoices look professional and your automated workflows function correctly.

The settings are divided into three main tabs: General, Subscription, and Payments.

General Tab

What it is: The General tab houses all the core details about your business, including contact information, banking details, and external service integrations.

Why it helps: The information entered here directly populates the header and footer of the PDFs generated for your customers. It also controls powerful automations like online payment collection and automatic email sending.

Basic Information

  • Organization Logo: Upload your company's logo. This will be prominently displayed at the top of all generated invoices.
  • Organization Name: Your registered business name.
  • GSTIN / UIN: Your tax identification number.
  • Complete Address, City, State, ZIP Code: Your physical billing address. (Note: The state selection is critical as it dictates whether CGST/SGST or IGST is applied to your invoices).
  • Phone Number & Email Address: Your primary business contact details.

Bank Details

These fields allow you to provide offline payment instructions to your customers directly on the invoice document.

  • Bank Name & Branch Name
  • Account Holder Name
  • Account Number
  • IFSC Code

Payment Gateway Integration

  • Integrate Payment Gateway (Toggle): Enable this to collect payments from your customers via Credit Card, Debit Card, or UPI directly through an online link.
  • Payment Provider: Currently supports Razorpay.
  • Key ID & Key Secret: The live API credentials provided by your payment gateway dashboard. Entering these links your Invoicey account to your merchant account.

Email Settings

  • Configure Email Settings (Toggle): Enable this to automatically send a "Thank You" email containing the attached PDF invoice to your customer the moment an invoice is generated.
  • SMTP Credentials (Host, Port, Username, Password): Your custom mail server settings (e.g., Gmail SMTP, Outlook, or custom domain). This ensures emails are sent from your own professional email address.

Danger Zone

  • Deactivate Organization: Temporarily suspends access for all users in your organization. This is useful if you are pausing business operations.
  • Delete Organization: Irreversible action. Permanently deletes your organization, users, customers, and all invoice data from the platform.

Subscription Tab

What it is: This tab displays the current status of your Invoicey software subscription.

Why it helps: It provides a quick overview of your plan limits and alerts you when your subscription is nearing expiration to prevent any disruption in your ability to generate invoices.

Available Information

  • Active Plan: The name of the tier you are currently subscribed to (e.g., Trial, Basic, Pro).
  • Status: Indicates whether your plan is currently ACTIVE or EXPIRED.
  • Expires On: The exact date your current billing cycle ends.
  • Features Included: A checklist reminding you of the capabilities unlocked by your current plan.
  • Subscribe Now / Extend Subscription: A quick-action button that takes you to the pricing page to renew or upgrade your plan.

Payments Tab

What it is: A historical ledger of all payments you have made to Invoicey for your software subscriptions.

Why it helps: It simplifies your internal accounting by providing easy access to past subscription invoices and receipts.

Available Information

  • Payment List: A chronological list of transactions showing the amount paid and the payment status (e.g., COMPLETED).
  • Transaction Details: Displays the date of the payment and the unique Transaction ID.
  • Download Receipt: Each entry features a download icon (⬇️) that generates a PDF receipt for that specific subscription payment, which you can save for your tax and accounting records.
