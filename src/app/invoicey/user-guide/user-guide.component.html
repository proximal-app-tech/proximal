Welcome to Invoicey. This guide will help you set up and manage your organization effectively.

person_add 1. Registration & Onboarding

To get started, you need to create an account for your organization.

1

Create Account: Navigate to the Registration screen.

2

User Information: Enter your Full Name, Email Address, and a strong Password.

3

Organization Details:

  • Organization Name: Your business's legal name.
  • Address: Complete business address.
  • GSTIN (Optional): If you are GST registered.
  • Location: Select your City, State, and ZIP Code.
4

Verification: Check your email for a verification link to activate your account.

inventory_2 2. Product Management (Product Master)

info

Pre-requisites: Product Categories and Tax Configuration.

A. Product Categories

Organize your items into groups (e.g., "Electronics", "Consulting Services"). Go to Admin > Product Categories and click "+".

B. Tax Configuration

Define GST rates (e.g., "GST 18%", "GST 5%"). The system automatically calculates CGST/SGST or IGST.

C. Managing Products

Go to Admin > Product Master to add items with HSN/SKU, Unit of Measure, and Pricing.

sell 3. Discount Management
