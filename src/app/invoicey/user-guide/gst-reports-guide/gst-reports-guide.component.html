receipt_long 14. GST Return Sheets Guide

The GST Return Sheets screen is a powerful tool to generate essential tax reports for filing your Goods and Services Tax (GST) returns. It supports both monthly summaries and annual return data extraction in CSV formats.

Key Features

  • Monthly & Yearly Views: Toggle between monthly and yearly reporting periods.
  • Summary Metrics: Instantly view critical metrics for the selected period:
    • Total Invoices generated
    • Taxable Turnover (Total sales excluding tax)
    • Total GST Collected
  • Export to CSV: Download ready-to-use spreadsheet files for your accountant or tax filing portal.
  • Invoice Preview: A quick table displaying up to 20 invoices from the selected period.

Generating Reports

Select your desired period using the Monthly / Yearly toggle and the Change date button. Then, choose one of the following reports to download:

1. GSTR-1 (Sales Statement)

  • Available in the Monthly view.
  • Generates a CSV file containing a detailed list of all outward supplies (sales) made during the month.
  • Useful for uploading your sales data to the GST portal.

2. GSTR-3B (Return Filing)

  • Available in the Monthly view.
  • Generates a summary CSV file required for your monthly self-declaration of GST liabilities.

3. GSTR-9 / GSTR-0 (Yearly Annual Summary)

  • Available in the Yearly view.
  • Extracts an annual summary of all transactions for the selected financial year to assist with annual return filing.
TIP: Make sure to review the "Summary Metrics" and "Invoice Preview" table before generating your reports to ensure the selected period contains the expected data. Returned or canceled invoices will appear with a strikethrough in the preview.
