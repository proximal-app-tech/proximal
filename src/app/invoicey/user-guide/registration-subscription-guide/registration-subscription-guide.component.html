person_add 1. Registration & Subscription Guide

Welcome to Invoicey! This guide will walk you through the process of creating your account, registering your organization, and selecting a subscription plan.

Registration Process

To get started, you need to create an account and set up your organization details. The registration form is divided into two main sections: User Information and Organization Details.

User Information:

This section collects your personal login credentials.

  • Full Name: Enter your complete name. This is a mandatory field.
  • Email Address: Provide a valid email address. This will be used to verify your account, send notifications, and log in to the application. The system will automatically check if the email is already registered.
  • Password: Create a strong password. It must meet the following criteria:
    • Minimum of 6 characters in length.
    • At least 1 uppercase letter (A-Z).
    • At least 1 lowercase letter (a-z).
    • At least 1 number (0-9).
    • At least 1 special symbol (e.g., !@#$%^&*).

Organization Details:

This section collects information about your business to ensure invoices and tax calculations are accurate.

  • Organization Name: The official or legal name of your business/company.
  • Address: The physical billing address of your organization.
  • GSTIN: Your 15-character Goods and Services Tax Identification Number (e.g., 22AAAAA0000A1Z5). Ensure this is accurate as it cannot be duplicated across multiple accounts.
  • City: The city where your organization is based.
  • State: Select your state from the provided dropdown list. This is crucial for calculating the correct GST type (CGST/SGST vs. IGST).
  • ZIP Code: Your area's postal/PIN code.
  • Country: This field is pre-filled as India and cannot be changed.

Completing Registration:

Before clicking the Complete Registration button:

  • Terms and Privacy Policy: You must check the box to agree to the Terms and Conditions and the Privacy Policy.
  • Once submitted, you will see a success message prompting you to check your email to verify your account.

2. Subscription Selection

After you have registered and verified your account, the next step is to choose a subscription plan tailored to your business needs.

Applying a Referral Code:

If you have a promotional or referral code:

  1. Locate the Enter Referral Code field at the top of the subscription screen.
  2. Enter your code and click Apply.
  3. If valid, a success message will appear showing your discount percentage, and the prices of all plans will automatically update to reflect the discount.

Choosing a Plan:

You will see a list of available plans. For each plan, you can review the following details:

  • Plan Name: The title of the subscription tier (e.g., Trial, Pro, Enterprise).
  • Base Price: The cost of the plan (discounted if a referral code was applied).
  • Tax Details: Taxes are automatically calculated and added to your final total.
    • If your organization's state is Tamil Nadu, it calculates GST 18% (CGST 9% + SGST 9%).
    • For all other states, it calculates IGST 18%.
  • Total Amount: The final amount payable, inclusive of all discounts and taxes.
  • Duration: The validity period of the plan in days.
  • Features: A checklist of the capabilities and limits included in that specific plan.

Making the Payment:

  1. Review the plan details and click Subscribe Now (or Get Started for free plans).
  2. Paid Plans: You will be securely redirected to our payment gateway (Razorpay). You can complete the transaction using various supported methods such as Credit/Debit Cards, UPI, or external wallets like Paytm.
  3. Free/Trial Plans: If the total amount is ₹0, the subscription will activate instantly without routing to the payment gateway.
  4. Upon a successful transaction, a confirmation message will appear, and you will be fully set up to use Invoicey!
