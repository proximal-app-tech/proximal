group 3. Manage User Guide

The Manage User feature in Invoicey allows you to invite multiple team members to your organization's account, assign specific roles, and manage their access securely.

Why and How This Helps

  • Team Collaboration: Instead of sharing a single login credential across your business, you can provide each team member with their own secure account.
  • Access Control: By utilizing Roles, you can ensure that sensitive administrative settings remain protected while your staff members focus on day-to-day operations like invoicing and billing.
  • Streamlined Operations: You can dynamically add, edit, or remove access as your team changes, ensuring your organization's data is always secure.

Understanding Roles

When adding a user to your organization, you determine their level of access. There are two primary roles in Invoicey:

1. Administrator (Admin)

  • Access: Full, unrestricted access to the application.
  • Capabilities: Can manage organization settings, view the complete business dashboard, subscribe to plans, and access the Manage User screen to add, edit, or delete other users.

2. Staff Member

  • Access: Restricted access focused on daily operations.
  • Capabilities: Full access to billing workflows, creating invoices, and basic customer management. They cannot access system settings, subscription billing, or manage other users.

How to Add a New User

To add a new team member to your organization, follow these steps:

  1. Navigate to the Manage User screen from your Admin Dashboard.
  2. Click the + icon (on mobile) or the ADD NEW USER button (on larger screens).
  3. Fill in the required details in the User Form:
    • Full Name: The user's complete name.
    • Email Address: The email they will use to log in.
    • Phone Number: Their primary contact number.
    • Password: Create an initial password for them.
    • Administrator Access: Toggle this switch ON if they require Admin privileges, or leave it OFF for standard Staff access.
  4. Click CREATE to finalize and add the user. The new user can immediately log in using the provided email and password.
NOTE - Subscription Limits: If your organization is on a Trial plan, you may be limited to a maximum of 1 user (the original account owner). To unlock the ability to add more team members, you will need to upgrade to a premium subscription plan.

Managing Existing Users

As an Administrator, you can also modify existing accounts from the Manage User screen:

  • Edit Details: Click the edit icon (or select the user on desktop) to update their Full Name, Phone Number, or promote/demote their Administrator access level. (Note: The email address cannot be changed after creation).
  • Delete User: Click the delete (trash bin) icon to completely remove a user's access to your organization. This action cannot be undone.
