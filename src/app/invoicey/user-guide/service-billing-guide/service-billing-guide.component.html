receipt_long Service Billing View - User Guide

Overview

The Service Billing View is designed specifically for creating invoices for non-physical items or professional services. Unlike product billing, service billing does not track inventory quantities and uses SAC (Service Accounting Codes) instead of HSN codes.

Key Sections

1. Client/Customer Details

Capture the details of the client receiving the service:

  • Phone Number: Enter the 10-digit phone number. Existing clients will be automatically looked up and their details autofilled.
  • Customer Name: Enter the full name or company name of the client.
  • Email & Address: Record contact and billing information.
  • State: Crucial for accurate GST calculation (determines whether CGST/SGST or IGST applies).
  • SEZ Zone: Mark this if the client operates in a Special Economic Zone, which will apply a zero GST rate to the services.
  • Save Customer Info: Check this to store the client's information in your database for future service bills.

2. Add Services

Add the professional services provided:

  • Add Service Item Button: Select from your pre-configured list of services.
  • Service Details: Each service automatically includes its predefined unit price, SAC code, and associated tax rate.
  • Adjusting Values: You can modify the unit price or quantity (hours, days, units of service) as needed for the specific bill.

3. Discounts & Miscellaneous

  • Automatic Discounts: If a service belongs to a discounted category or has a direct discount assigned, it will be automatically calculated and applied.
  • Miscellaneous Charges: You can add any extra miscellaneous fees (e.g., travel expenses, convenience fees, tips) before finalizing the total.

4. Holding a Service Bill

  • Pause Icon: If you need to step away or gather more details from the client before finalizing the bill, use the pause icon (top right) to hold the current session.
  • Held Bills Banner: You will see an alert banner indicating how many held bills you have. You can restore these drafts anytime without losing data.

5. Finalizing & Payment

  • Proceed to Payment: Once all services and details are confirmed, click to proceed.
  • Payment Processing: Record the payment against the service invoice. You can log Cash, Online payments via Razorpay, or mark the invoice as unpaid/partially paid if the client is paying later.

Help & Tips

  • Use the Help Icon (?) for an on-screen summary of the billing steps.
  • Always ensure the correct State and SEZ status are selected before adding services to guarantee accurate tax breakdown on the final invoice.
