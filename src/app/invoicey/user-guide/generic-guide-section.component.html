inventory_2 3. Product Management (Product Master)

info

Pre-requisites: Product Categories and Tax Configuration.

A. Product Categories

Organize your items into groups (e.g., "Electronics", "Consulting Services"). Go to Admin > Product Categories and click "+".

B. Tax Configuration

Define GST rates (e.g., "GST 18%", "GST 5%"). The system automatically calculates CGST/SGST or IGST.

C. Managing Products

Go to Admin > Product Master to add items with HSN/SKU, Unit of Measure, and Pricing.

sell 4. Discount Management

Create promo codes (e.g., "HOLIDAY20") with percentage discounts, usage limits, and expiry dates.

group 5. User Management

Collaborate by adding team members with specific roles:

  • Administrator: Full access.
  • Staff: Access to billing and basic customer management.

payments 6. Subscriptions & Payments

Manage access and view financial history under Admin > Organization Settings.

receipt_long 7. Quotations & Invoices

Quotation Workflow

Draft → Customer Approved/Rejected → Generate Invoice

Invoice History

Search by Invoice Number or Customer Name in Billing > Invoice List.

Pending Payments

Track PENDING or PARTIAL payments and record receipts (Cash, Card, UPI).

contact_page 8. CRM (Customers & Vendors)

Maintain a complete database of business contacts with lifetime value metrics and quick contact actions.

analytics 9. Declarations & Reporting

Manage invoice declarations and export GST Return Sheets (GSTR-1, GSTR-3B) as CSV files.
