category Product Category Guide

The Category Creation screen allows you to group your products or services into distinct classifications. Properly categorizing your inventory makes it easier to filter, search, and manage your items, as well as providing more organized reports.

Here is a detailed breakdown of each field on the category creation screen:

1. Category Name

Required

This is the primary identifier for your category (e.g., Electronics, Consulting Services, Food & Beverage).

  • Purpose: To provide a clear, recognizable name for grouping related products or services together.
  • Usage: Enter a concise, capitalized name. This name will appear across the app whenever you are filtering or selecting items.

2. Description

Optional

A brief explanation of what types of items belong in this specific category.

  • Purpose: Helps provide context, especially useful if you have multiple staff members managing your inventory or if your categories are broad.
  • Usage: Add a short sentence describing the contents, such as "All physical electronic goods and accessories" or "Hourly consulting and labor charges".

3. Auto Discount Code

Optional

This dropdown allows you to link a pre-existing active discount to this specific category.

  • Purpose: To automatically apply promotional logic to all items that fall under this category.
  • Usage: Select an active discount code from the list. Once selected, any item categorized under this group will automatically inherit the associated discount rules. Note that you must first create discount codes in the Discounts section of Product Settings before they will appear in this list.
