receipt_long Invoice History (Invoice List) User Guide

The Invoice History screen (also known as the Invoice List screen) is your centralized hub for viewing and managing all invoices generated within Invoicey. It supports both Product and Service invoices and offers a range of tools for searching, reviewing details, managing returns, and sharing invoices with your customers.

1. Navigating the Invoice List

The main view of this screen displays a list of all your generated invoices, sorted chronologically.

  • Invoice Cards: Each card in the list displays the customer's name, invoice number, total amount, date of creation, and due date (if applicable).
  • Tags: Invoices are clearly tagged as either PRODUCT or SERVICE for quick identification.
  • Status Badges: If an invoice has been processed for a return, it will display a RETURNED (fully returned) or PARTIAL RETURN badge.

2. Key Features and Functions

Search functionality

You can quickly find specific invoices using the search bar at the top of the screen.

  • Search by Name or Number: Type in either the Invoice Number or the Customer's Name to instantly filter the list.
  • Clear Search: Click the "X" (clear) button inside the search field to reset the list and view all invoices.

View Details

To view the full details of an invoice, simply click or tap on it from the list.

  • Desktop/Tablet: The details will open in a dedicated reading pane on the right side of the screen.
  • Mobile: The details will slide up from the bottom of the screen in a detailed view.

Scan Invoice Barcode

If you have a printed invoice with a barcode, you can instantly pull it up in the app.

  • Click the Barcode Scanner Icon located in the top-right corner of the app bar.
  • Use your device's camera to scan the barcode, and the app will automatically fetch and display the corresponding invoice details.

Print and Download PDF

From the Invoice Details view, you have options to generate physical or digital copies.

  • Print/View PDF: Click the print/view option to generate a standard PDF format of the original invoice for printing or downloading.
  • Print Adjusted (Returns): If an invoice has been partially returned, you will have the option to print an "Adjusted Invoice" that reflects the updated quantities and totals after the return.

Email Invoice

If your organization has enabled and configured Email Settings, you can email invoices directly to your customers.

  • In the Invoice Details view, click the Email button to instantly send the digital invoice PDF to the customer's email address on file.

Return Invoice (Product Invoices Only)

If a customer needs to return items due to damage, exchange, or receiving the wrong item, you can process returns directly from this screen.

  • Select the product invoice from the list and click the Return button.
  • Specify Items and Quantities: A dialog will appear listing all eligible items. Enter the exact quantity being returned (the system prevents returning more than the original sold amount).
  • Select Reason: Choose a reason for the return (e.g., Damaged, Exchange, Wrong Item).
  • Process Refund: Verify the calculated "Total Refund Amount" and select the transfer method (Cash or Online) used to refund the customer.
  • Note: Returning items will automatically update the invoice status to "Partial Return" or "Returned" and adjust your financial records.

Pull-to-Refresh

To ensure you are looking at the most up-to-date information, you can refresh the invoice list.

  • Simply pull down on the invoice list (or scroll to the top and swipe down) to fetch the latest product and service invoices from the server.

Help & Guidance

  • If you ever need a quick reminder of the screen's features, click the Help (?) Icon in the top right corner to display an on-screen summary.
