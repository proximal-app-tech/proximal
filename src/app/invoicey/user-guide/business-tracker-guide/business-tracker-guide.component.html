account_balance_wallet 13. Business Accounts Tracker Guide

The Business Accounts Tracker serves as a digital cashbook and general ledger for your organization. It allows you to monitor all inward and outward cash flows, maintaining a running balance over time.

Key Features

  • Automated Logging: Whenever you record a non-cash invoice payment (e.g., Card or UPI), it automatically appears here as an incoming transaction.
  • Manual Record Entry: Add custom income (sales, rent, investments) or expenses (utilities, salaries, supplies) directly.
  • Running Balance: View your organization's financial health with an automatically calculated running balance for each transaction.
  • Monthly & Yearly Tracking: Toggle between monthly and yearly views to analyze your cash flow over different timeframes.
  • Export to CSV: Download your ledger to a spreadsheet using the download icon in the top right corner.

Adding a Manual Record

  1. Click the Add Record floating action button at the bottom right.
  2. Select the Transaction Date.
  3. Enter a short Description (e.g., "Office Internet Bill", "Walk-in Sales").
  4. Define the Category (e.g., "Utilities", "Sales").
  5. Enter the amount in the appropriate field:
    • Cash In (+): Use this for money received (income).
    • Cash Out (-): Use this for money spent (expenses).
  6. Click SAVE RECORD.

Editing or Deleting Records

  • Edit: To modify an existing manual entry, locate the transaction and click the blue Edit (pencil) icon. Update the necessary fields and save.
  • Delete: If an entry was made in error, click the red Delete (trash) icon next to the transaction to remove it permanently. The running balance will automatically recalculate.
